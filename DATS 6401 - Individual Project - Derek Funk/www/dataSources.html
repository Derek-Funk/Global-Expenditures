World Bank

This project relies entirely on the World Bank for data. The World Bank's open data collection has a wide variety of data sets on global development.

The main reason for deciding to use just one data source was to avoid data inconsistencies that might occur from pulling from multiple sources with different collection methods. However, this also meant that certain nations and years were omitted from this project due to large amounts of missing data from the World Bank. This restricts the analysis of global spending on education, healthcare, and the military to the years 2010-2015, and to the following nations:

  • Australia
  • Brazil
  • France
  • Germany
  • Indonesia
  • Italy
  • Japan
  • Mexico
  • South Africa
  • South Korea
  • United Kingdom
  • United States
